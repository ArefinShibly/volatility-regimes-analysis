# Volatility Regimes & Currency Correlations (2015-2024)

**Key Findings:**
- 66.7% of pairs (44/66) show statistically significant correlation shifts (p < 0.05)
- USD pairs: positive correlations **increase** during high volatility
- Gold/silver: correlations become **negative** (safe-haven effect)

|------------------|----------------|-----------------|----------|

| Gold vs USD      | 0.15           | -0.23           | <0.01    |
